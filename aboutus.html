About Us

What began as a college project evolved into a comprehensive website initiated by four classmates. Specializing in services for new homes, the platform focuses on modular kitchens and various home essentials. This student-driven venture seamlessly transitioned from academia to reality, showcasing the team's dedication. Now a full-fledged website, it provides a one-stop solution for individuals seeking top-notch modular kitchen and home services, embodying the collaborative spirit and entrepreneurial drive of the original four classmates.
